James Braidwood

1800 to 1861

James Braidwood formed the first government paid town fire department, the Edinburgh Fire Engine Establishment, in 1824. Braidwood had a major impact in the science of modern day firefighting.

In 1833, the London Fire Engine Establishment was formed and Braidwood was named the first Fire Chief.
